About Me

Welcome to CrushMe, your number one source for all things you want. We're dedicated to giving you the best shopping experience, with a focus on product quality, Customer Support, Free and Fast Delivery.

Founded in 2021 by Lakshit, CrushMe has come a long way from its beginnings in Haryana, India. When Lakshit first started out, his passion for serving the best quality products drove him to build this app so that CrushMe can offer you the best products. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own app/website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
